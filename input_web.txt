Introduction

Printing technology plays a crucial role in modern communication, business operations, education, and industrial processes. Over the years, printing systems have evolved significantly, moving from traditional impact printing methods to more advanced non-impact technologies such as inkjet, laser, and thermal printing. Each of these technologies differs in terms of printing mechanism, cost structure, efficiency, print quality, and suitability for specific applications. As organizations and individuals increasingly seek cost-effective and efficient solutions, the question of whether to convert from one printing technology to another has become increasingly relevant.
